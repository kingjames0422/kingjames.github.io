Jazz Residences

Jazz Residences is a 4-tower “vertical village” at the heart of Bel-Air Makati that boasts of five-star amenities. It also has its own mall – the Jazz Mall, complete with commercial and food establishments, and an SM Hypermarket that can provide all the conveniences at your doorstep.

Address:

Nicanor Garcia, Makati, Metro Manila, Philippines

Landmarks

While Jazz Residences is just a stone’s throw away from the Makati Central Business District, it also transports you to Reposo’s artistic hub – a quaint and laid-back neighborhood with an array of unique restaurants, designs and curio shops. Cultural activities and festivities are some of the highlights that abound in this artistic side of Makati.
