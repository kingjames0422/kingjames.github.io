Princeton Residences

With hotel-inspired interiors and luxurious amenities, Princeton Residences offers and ideal lifestyle of elegance and sophistication. Convenience is also given, with the LRT 2 Gilmore Station right beside the development, and the various dining establishments in Greenhills and Tomas Morato within close proximity. At this location, the choices are plenty, and practicality is also considered, with schools, commercial centers and hospital nearby.

Address:

Beside LRT 2 Gilmore Station (South Entrance) 1112, Aurora Blvd, Quezon City, Metro Manila, Philippines

Landmarks

Right beside the LRT 2 Gilmore station, the development is highly accessible by commute and easily navigated by car. This means Princeton Residences homeowners will easily reach noted establishments such as the Greenhills Shopping Center, the famed restaurants of Tomas Morato and the Ortigas business district. Important Institutions are also nearby such as St. Paul University, La Salle Greenhills and St. Luke’s Medical Center. Moreover, fitness and recreational establishment can also be found within the area.
