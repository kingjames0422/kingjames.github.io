Shore 3 Residences

Shore 3 Residences exudes exceptional living environment that reflects its unmistakable coastal surroundings. Taking inspiration from Wharf-front Lifestyle, nautical-themed amenities add a distinct personality to the development, making it an enviable marina haven. Boardwalks connect these to the luxe lounge areas, an outdoor gym circuit and lush gardens which complete this exclusive modern residential resort. Welcome to an exceptional living environment where your love for anything nautical is at full display.

Address:

Lot 3-C, Seaside Boulevard and Sunrise Drive, Mall of Asia Complex, Pasay City, Philippines

Landmarks

Shore 3 Residences is located right inside the ever-growing SM Mall of Asia Complex. It captures the conveniences of modern city living by being right in the heart of an exciting and vibrant integrated Lifestyle District. The Mall of Asia central business district is home to the offices of the best established and start-up companies of var ying industries.

Transportation

Traveling to and from Shore 3 is a breeze with the abundance of transport hubs found in the area. Whether you are up for a weekend mall stroll or a fine dining experience by the bay, you are never far from your home.

Business District

Feel the world at your fingertips as the Entertainment City, a place full of the finest options in leisure, is right beside it. The presence of hotels and conventions centers as well as its close proximity to domestic and international airports attract businessmen and investors.
