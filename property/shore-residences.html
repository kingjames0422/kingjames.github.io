Shore Residences

Conveniently located at the SM Mall of Asia Complex in Pasay City, Shore Residences offers the finest in resort living, with an exclusive beach-like amenity right at the heart of the development. The four towers that comprise Shore Residences are spread on a total of 3.3 hectares at a prime property in Pasay City. Each building soars 19 storeys high, affording residents impressive views of the soulful Manila Bay and its surrounding cityscape.

Address:

Seaside Blvd cor. Sunrise Drive, Mall of Asia Complex, Seaside Blvd., Pasay 1300, Metro Manila, Philippines

Landmarks

Everything is within reach – from the numerous shops and dining spots at SM Mall of Asia, to the thriving array of activities and attractions in the Entertainment City. It is also near major roads and highways such as EDSA, Macapagal Avenue, and Roxas Boulevard, so going from one city to another is a total breeze.

Transportation

Imagine coming home to paradise everyday. It is now possible with Shore Residences. It is strategically built near the vibrant business and entertainment district of Pasay City. Shore Residences is located right inside the ever-growing SM Mall of Asia Complex. It captures the conveniences of modern city living by being right in the heart of an exciting and vibrant integrated Lifestyle District.

Business District

The Mall of Asia central business district is home to the offices of the best established and start-up companies of varying industries. Feel the world at your fingertips as the Entertainment City, a place full of the finest options in leisure, is right beside it. The presence of hotels and conventions centers as well as its close proximity to domestic and international airports attract businessmen and investors. Traveling to and from Shore is a breeze with the abundance of transport hubs found in the area. Whether you are up for a weekend mall stroll or a fine dining experience by the bay, you are never far from your home.

Amenities

Shore Residences captures luxurious lifestyle distinguished by its wharf-inspired amenities. Elegant features and carefully planned structures are found within the development. Be transported to world-famous wharf-front destinations when you lounge by the pool. Boardwalks connect these to the luxe lounge areas, an outdoor gym circuit and lush gardens which complete this exclusive modern residential resort. Welcome to an exceptional living environment where your love for anything nautical is at full display.
