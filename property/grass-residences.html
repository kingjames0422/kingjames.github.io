Grass Residences

Rest and recreation has never been more built-in. Located in the lively northside of the city, Grass Residences is a 5-tower development that is literally a hop, skip and bridge way from SM North EDSA.

Address:

Nueva Viscaya St, Bago Bantay, Quezon City, Metro Manila, Philippines

Landmarks

SM City North Edsa is the established lifestyle district north of Metro Manila and is the world’s 3rd largest shopping mall. Grass Residences Complex, completes the full integration of this exciting and dynamic lifestyle district in the Quezon City. Built with an exclusive bridge way connected to SM City North Edsa, residents enjoy instant access to all the good things offered in the lifestyle district. Enjoy 800 retail shops, over 200 restaurant and dining options, 12 cinemas including an IMAX theatre.Enjoy the convenience of staying within proximity of the top schools, hospitals, malls and other institutions that enable you to live the conveniences of modern life. Cherish time with family as you unwind in a verdant setting of more than a hectare of open greens, giving you plenty of room for leisure and play.
