Berkeley Residences

Earn your degree in comfortable living at Berkeley Residences, in the heart of Quezon City’s prestigious university belt. Located along Katipunan Avenue, right across Miriam College and Ateneo De Manila University, Berkeley Residences is within walking distance to various specialty restaurants and shopping centers.

Address:

J Escaler, Katipunan Ave. Quezon City, 1800 Metro Manila, Philippines

Landmarks

Berkeley Residences is close to the premiere colleges and universities of Metro Manila such as Ateneo De Manila University, University of the Philippines, and Miriam College.

Business District

This high-rise residential development has stunning views of Antipolo, Quezon City, and Ortigas skyline. It has an amenity floor complete with swimming pools, function rooms, children’s playground, a student’s lounge and a chill-out deck.
