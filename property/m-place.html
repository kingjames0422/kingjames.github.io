MPlace South Triangle

Developed by SM Development Corporation (SMDC), M Place South Triangle is a high-rise four-tower residential condominium featuring 23 residential levels with a floor area of 20 to 40 square meters each M Place is built to cater to the young market who wants a place of their own to complement their rising careers and start the path towards independent living.

Address:

Panay Ave and 1103, Mo. Ignacia Ave, Diliman, Quezon City, Metro Manila, Philippines

Landmarks

Nestled in the heart of "Media City" is MPlace South Triangle, the perfect home for young professionals working in Quezon City. Near dining establishments, commercial districts, and health centers, this conveniently located condo development suits an individual on the go. Enjoy access to jogging paths, fitness stations and swimming pools for leisure, and feel like a star as you step in the Hollywood-star-themed lobbies.

Transportation

Being at the heart of the South Triangle area gives M Place residents the advantage of easy access to the most sought after dining places, bars, entertainment centers and Bars in Timog, as well as to a host of the best beauty salons in the Metro. With all forms of public transportation available 24/7, getting from one place to another either for leisure or business purposes is never difficult.

Amenities

M Place South Triangle units offer a variety of unit sizes, 16 sqm, 20 sqm, 4 sqm, 30 sqm and 40 sqm. Residents can choose from studio-type units, studio-type units with garden, one-bedroom units, one-bedroom L-type units and two-bedroom units.

  • Children's Play Area
