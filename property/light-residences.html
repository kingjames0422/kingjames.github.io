Light Residences

For those who rejoice in a fast-paced lifestyle where convenience and ease are prized, Light Residences brings you to the center of the action. Located along EDSA and Madison Street,it sits at an advantageous location, linked to BONI-EDSA MRT where you can access any point of Manila. Decked out in urban conveniences, Light Residences is a joy to live in. For those who find shopping irresistible, Light Residences has its very own Light Mall. Enjoy spur-of-the-moment movie dates in any of its digital cinemas, or do a quick stop at its own Savemore Market.

Address:

Boni Station MRT, Mandaluyong, Metro Manila, Philippines

Landmarks

Perfect for people-on-the-go, Light Residences is strategically located at Madison St. corner EDSA. It is conveniently linked to the Boni-EDSA MRT station that transports you to any point in Metro Manila.

Hot Spots

Light Residences’ podium would house the Light Mall, a hip lifestyle center that doubles as an extension of the homeowner’s living, dining and entertainment areas. Resident’s can sample a different cuisine every day at the mall’s wide array of restaurants, while the cinema is perfect for spontaneous movie dates. Those eager for some retail therapy can take advantage of the myriad fashion, home and specialty shops. For daily essentials, a quick stop at its very own Savemore Market completes the conveniences you can possible have in your own living space.
